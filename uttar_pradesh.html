Discover the Heritage of Uttar pradesh

From the majestic Taj Mahal to the spiritual ghats of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh offers a unique blend of culture, heritage, and natural beauty that will leave you spellbound.

Popular Destinations

Taj Mahal, Agra

The iconic white marble mausoleum and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Varanasi Ghats

The spiritual capital of India, where pilgrims perform rituals along the sacred Ganges River at sunrise and sunset.

Ayodhya

The birthplace of Lord Rama, featuring magnificent temples and a rich spiritual heritage.

Lucknow

The city of Nawabs, known for its refined etiquette, beautiful monuments, and delicious Awadhi cuisine.

Mathura-Vrindavan

The birthplace of Lord Krishna, featuring numerous temples and a vibrant spiritual atmosphere.

Dudhwa National Park

A tiger reserve with diverse wildlife including tigers, leopards, and the rare swamp deer.
