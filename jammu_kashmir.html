Popular Destinations

Srinagar

The summer capital of Jammu & Kashmir, famous for Dal Lake, Mughal Gardens, and houseboats that offer a unique stay experience.

Gulmarg

Known as the "Meadow of Flowers," this premier ski destination boasts the world's highest golf course and gondola ride.

Pahalgam

A picturesque valley known as the "Valley of Shepherds," offering breathtaking views and the starting point for Amarnath Yatra.

Jammu

The winter capital, known as the "City of Temples," featuring the sacred Vaishno Devi shrine and majestic Bahu Fort.

Leh-Ladakh

The land of high passes, featuring breathtaking landscapes, ancient monasteries, and unique Buddhist culture.

Patnitop

A beautiful hill station in the Jammu region, offering panoramic views of the Himalayas and lush green meadows.
