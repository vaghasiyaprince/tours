Discover Tamil Nadu

From ancient temples to hill stations, beaches to wildlife sanctuaries, Tamil Nadu offers diverse experiences for every traveler.

Explore Now

Popular Destinations

Chennai

The gateway to South India, Chennai blends traditional culture with modernity, featuring Marina Beach, Kapaleeshwarar Temple, and more.

Madurai

Home to the magnificent Meenakshi Amman Temple, Madurai is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.

Thanjavur

Famous for the Brihadeeswarar Temple (a UNESCO World Heritage Site) and its rich cultural heritage in art and music.

Ooty

The Queen of Hill Stations, known for its tea plantations, botanical gardens, and pleasant climate throughout the year.

Kanyakumari

Where three seas meet, famous for its spectacular sunrise and sunset views, and the Vivekananda Rock Memorial.

Mahabalipuram

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ancient rock-cut temples and sculptures by the Pallava dynasty.
