Discover the Vibrant Gujarat

From the majestic Statue of Unity to the white deserts of Kutch, Gujarat offers a unique blend of culture, heritage, and natural beauty that will leave you spellbound.

Explore Now

Popular Destinations

Statue of Unity

The world's tallest statue dedicated to India's Iron Man, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, standing at 182 meters tall.

Rann of Kutch

The white salt desert that transforms into a cultural extravaganza during the Rann Utsav festival.

Gir National Park

The only natural habitat of the Asiatic lions in the world, spread across 1412 square kilometers.

Somnath Temple

One of the 12 Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva, located along the coastline of the Arabian Sea.

Dwarkadhish Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, located in the holy city of Dwarka.

Girnar

Girnar is a sacred mountain range and a major pilgrimage site for both Hindus and Jains, located in the Junagadh District
